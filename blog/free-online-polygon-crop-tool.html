How to Use Polygon Crop

Upload your image. Select a preset shape or create a custom polygon by clicking to place points. Drag points to adjust shape positioning and size. The tool shows live preview with transparent backgrounds or custom background colors. Download as PNG with transparency for layering in design software or on websites.

The tool maintains image quality during cropping, using high-resolution processing that preserves detail and clarity. Anti-aliasing ensures smooth edges without pixelation, even for complex custom shapes.

Design Tip: When creating custom polygon crops, use fewer points for cleaner shapes. Complex polygons with many tight angles can look messy. Simple, bold shapes have maximum visual impact.
