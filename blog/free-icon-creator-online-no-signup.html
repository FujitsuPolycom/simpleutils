Stock icons rarely match your exact needs or brand identity. Custom icons help your application stand out, communicate functionality clearly, and maintain visual consistency. How to Design Icons Online

Start with a canvas size matching your needs. Add shapes like circles, squares, triangles, or stars. Layer them to create complex designs. Apply colors, gradients, shadows, and borders. Add text with customizable fonts and effects. The tool provides real-time preview, showing exactly how your icon will look.

The layer system lets you organize elements, reorder them, and adjust individual components without starting over. This makes iterative design easy - try different color combinations, adjust sizing, or experiment with effects until you achieve the perfect look.

Design Tip: Icons should be simple and recognizable at small sizes. Avoid thin lines and fine details. Use bold shapes and high contrast for maximum clarity, especially for mobile app icons.

Icon Design Best Practices

Effective icons communicate their purpose instantly. Use universally recognized symbols when possible - a magnifying glass for search, a gear for settings, a house for home. Maintain consistent style across icon sets using the same color palette, stroke weights, and corner radius.

For app icons, follow platform guidelines: iOS favors rounded squares with gradients, Android uses adaptive icons with dynamic shapes, and web favicons work best with simple, high-contrast designs.
