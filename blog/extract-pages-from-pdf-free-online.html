How to Extract PDF Pages

Upload your PDF document. The tool displays thumbnail previews of all pages with page numbers. Select pages to extract by clicking thumbnails, entering page ranges (e.g., "1-5, 10, 15-20"), or choosing extraction patterns (odd pages, even pages, every nth page).

Efficiency Tip: Use page range notation for fast extraction. "1-10" extracts pages 1 through 10. "1, 5, 9" extracts only those specific pages. "2-" extracts page 2 through the end.
