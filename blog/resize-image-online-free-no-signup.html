How to Resize Images

Upload your image or drag and drop it into the tool. Enter target width and height in pixels, or resize by percentage. Choose whether to maintain aspect ratio (preventing distortion) or stretch to exact dimensions. The tool shows real-time preview and file size estimates before you download.

For maintaining image quality, the tool uses high-quality resampling algorithms. Downscaling larger images produces sharp results, while upscaling uses interpolation to minimize quality loss.

Quality Tip: Downscaling (making images smaller) maintains quality well. Upscaling (enlarging) works best for modest increases - doubling size is usually fine, but 10x enlargement will show quality degradation.

Common Resize Scenarios

Social Media: Each platform has optimal image sizes. Instagram posts work best at 1080×1080px, Facebook covers at 820×312px, Twitter headers at 1500×500px.
