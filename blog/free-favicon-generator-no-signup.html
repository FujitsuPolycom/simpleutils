A favicon is the small icon that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and mobile home screens. This free favicon generator no signup tool converts any image into all the favicon formats you need - from tiny 16×16 browser icons to 512×512 PWA icons - in seconds.

Why Your Website Needs a Favicon

Favicons make your website instantly recognizable in crowded browser tab bars. Users navigating between dozens of tabs rely on favicons to identify your site at a glance. Professional websites must have favicons - missing favicons look unfinished and harm credibility.

Modern web applications need multiple icon sizes: classic 16×16 and 32×32 for browser tabs, 180×180 for iOS home screens, 192×192 and 512×512 for Android and Progressive Web Apps, and various sizes for Windows tiles. Manually creating all these sizes is tedious. This free favicon generator handles everything automatically.

How to Generate Favicons

Upload your logo, icon, or any image. The tool automatically resizes and optimizes it for all required favicon formats. You'll get ICO files for classic browsers, PNG files for modern web apps, and the HTML code to add to your website's head section.

The generator handles transparency correctly, ensuring your favicon looks good on light and dark browser themes. It also previews how your favicon will appear at different sizes, so you can verify readability before downloading.

Design Tip: Simple designs work best for favicons. Complex logos with fine details become unreadable at 16×16 pixels. Use high-contrast colors and minimal shapes for maximum clarity.

Supported Platforms and Formats

The tool generates favicons for all major platforms: standard web browsers (favicon.ico), Apple iOS devices (apple-touch-icon.png), Android Chrome (manifest icons), Microsoft Windows tiles, and Safari pinned tabs. You get a complete package ready to upload to your website.

PWA and App Icon Requirements

Progressive Web Apps require manifest.json files with icon declarations. The free favicon generator no signup tool creates properly formatted manifests and all required icon sizes, making your website PWA-ready. Users can add your app to their home screens with professional-looking icons.
