How to Crop Images Online

Upload your image or drag and drop it into the tool. Select a preset aspect ratio (1:1 square, 16:9 widescreen, 4:3 standard, etc.) or define custom dimensions. Drag the crop box to position it perfectly, then resize by dragging corners. The tool shows a live preview of your cropped result.

Advanced features include rotate and flip options, so you can correct orientation before cropping. Grid overlays help align crops using the rule of thirds for more aesthetically pleasing compositions.
