Fillable forms eliminate printing, handwriting, and scanning. Recipients fill them digitally, producing legible, professional results. Form Field Types

Text Fields: Single-line or multi-line text input for names, addresses, comments, or any text data.

Checkboxes: Yes/no questions, agreement confirmations, or multiple-choice selections.

Radio Buttons: Single selection from multiple options (gender, agreement choice, rating level).

Dropdown Lists: Space-efficient selection from long option lists (states, countries, departments).

Date Fields: Formatted date inputs with calendar pickers for consistent date entry.

Signature Fields: Digital signature areas for contracts, agreements, and authorization forms.

How to Create PDF Forms

Upload your existing PDF document. Click to add form fields at specific positions. Drag to size fields appropriately. Configure properties: field names, default values, required status, and validation rules. The tool shows a live preview of your fillable form.

For complex forms, the tool supports field calculations (automatic sum of invoice line items), conditional visibility (showing additional fields based on previous answers), and data validation (ensuring emails contain @ symbols, phone numbers match patterns).

Design Tip: Clearly label all form fields. Use consistent sizing and alignment for professional appearance. Mark required fields with asterisks. Test your form before distribution to ensure all fields work correctly.
