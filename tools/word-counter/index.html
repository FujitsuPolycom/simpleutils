Frequently Asked Questions

How is word count calculated?

Words are counted by splitting text on whitespace and punctuation. Hyphenated words count as one word. Numbers and special characters are excluded unless they're part of a word.

What reading speed is used for time estimates?

Reading time is calculated at 200 words per minute, which is the average reading speed for adults. Speaking time uses 130 words per minute, the average speaking pace for presentations and speeches.

What is keyword density analysis?

Keyword density shows how often specific words appear in your text. This helps identify overused words and ensures natural keyword distribution for SEO. Common words (stop words) like "the" and "and" are excluded from the analysis.

How accurate is the character count?

Character count includes all visible characters, spaces, and punctuation. The "characters without spaces" count excludes only space characters. Both counts are accurate for word processors, social media limits, and content management systems.
