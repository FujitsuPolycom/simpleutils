Frequently Asked Questions

Is this privacy policy legally binding?

This generator creates a template based on common practices. While it covers many legal requirements, you should have a lawyer review it to ensure compliance with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction.

Do I need a privacy policy for my website?

Yes, if you collect any personal data from visitors (emails, cookies, analytics, etc.), privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA require you to have a privacy policy explaining how you collect, use, and protect that data.

What's the difference between GDPR and CCPA?

GDPR is a European Union regulation that applies to all EU residents. CCPA is a California law that applies to California residents. Both give users rights over their personal data, but have different specific requirements.

Can I edit the generated policy?

Absolutely! This is a starting template. You can (and should) customize it to match your specific business practices and have it reviewed by legal counsel.
