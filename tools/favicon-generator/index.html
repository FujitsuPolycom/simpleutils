Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do I need for a modern website?

Modern websites should include 16x16 and 32x32 for browser tabs, 48x48 for desktop shortcuts, and 180x180 for Apple devices. Our generator creates all these sizes automatically.

What's the difference between favicon and Apple touch icon?

Favicons appear in browser tabs and bookmarks. Apple touch icons are used when someone saves your website to their iPhone or iPad home screen. They're larger (180x180) and display as app-like icons.

Should I use PNG or ICO format?

PNG is now the standard and widely supported by all modern browsers. It offers better quality and transparency support compared to the older ICO format. Our generator creates PNG files for maximum compatibility.

Why do my favicons look blurry at small sizes?

Complex logos or detailed images often don't scale well to 16x16 pixels. For best results, use simple designs with bold shapes and high contrast. Consider creating a simplified version of your logo specifically for favicons.
