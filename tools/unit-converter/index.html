Unit Converter

Convert between units instantly with our comprehensive unit converter. Switch between length, weight, temperature, speed, area, volume, and digital storage units. Enter a value and see all conversions at once in an easy-to-read grid format.

How to Use

  1. Select a category (Length, Weight, Temperature, etc.)
  2. Enter the value you want to convert
  3. Choose the unit you're converting from using the dropdown
  4. See all conversions for that category displayed instantly in the grid below
  5. Switch categories anytime to convert different types of measurements

Frequently Asked Questions

What units are supported?

We support common units in seven categories: Length (meter, kilometer, mile, yard, foot, inch, centimeter), Weight (kilogram, gram, pound, ounce, ton), Temperature (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin), Speed (km/h, mph, m/s, knots), Area, Volume, and Digital Storage (byte to terabyte).

How accurate are the conversions?

All conversions use standard, internationally recognized conversion factors. Results are accurate to multiple decimal places. For most practical purposes, the precision is more than sufficient.

Why does the tool show all conversions at once?

Showing all conversions simultaneously lets you quickly compare different units without multiple clicks. It's especially useful when you're not sure which unit you need or want to see multiple equivalents at a glance.

Can I convert between different categories?

No, you can only convert within the same category (e.g., length to length, weight to weight). Converting between different types of measurements (like length to weight) isn't physically meaningful without additional context.
