How to Use

  1. Fill in your business information (name, address, email, and optional logo)
  2. Enter your client's details (name, address, email)
  3. Set invoice number, dates, and payment terms
  4. Add line items by clicking "+ Add Item" - describe each service/product with quantity and price
  5. Set your tax rate (if applicable) - calculations update automatically
  6. Add any notes or payment terms at the bottom
  7. Click "Download Invoice PDF" to generate and save your professional invoice

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save my invoice to edit later?

Currently, the tool generates invoices on-demand without saving. For repeated invoices to the same client, consider keeping a template document with your standard information filled in.

What format is the downloaded invoice?

The invoice is generated as a PDF file, which is the standard format for professional invoices. PDFs can be easily emailed and are universally readable.

Can I customize the invoice design?

The current version uses a professional template. If you need custom designs, you can use the generated PDF as a reference and recreate it in design software like Canva or Adobe Illustrator.

Is this tool suitable for legal invoicing?

Yes, the invoices include all standard elements (invoice number, dates, line items, totals). However, check your local regulations for any specific requirements (like tax IDs or registration numbers) and add them to the notes section.

All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
