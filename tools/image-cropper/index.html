How to Use the Image Cropper

  1. Click "Choose File" and select the image you want to crop
  2. Drag the corners or edges of the crop box to select the area you want to keep
  3. Move the crop box by dragging from inside the selection area
  4. Click "Crop Image" to apply the crop
  5. Download your cropped image or click "Reset" to start over with a different selection

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I crop images to specific aspect ratios?

This tool allows free-form cropping, giving you complete control over the crop area. You can manually adjust the selection to match common aspect ratios like 16:9, 4:3, or 1:1 (square).

Does cropping reduce image quality?

No! Cropping removes portions of the image but doesn't compress or degrade the quality of the remaining area. The pixels you keep stay at their original quality.

What file formats can I crop?

Our image cropper supports all standard formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP. The cropped image is saved in PNG format to preserve quality.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Most modern devices can handle images up to 20-30MB without issues.

All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
