Contact Us

Have a question, suggestion, or found a bug? We'd love to hear from you.

Get in Touch

The best way to reach us is by email. Whether it's a quick question or a detailed feature request, send it our way:

hello@simpleutils.org

We typically respond within 1–2 business days. During busy periods it may take a bit longer, but every message gets read.

What We'd Love to Hear About

  • 🐛 Bug reports — Something not working right? Let us know what tool, what browser you're using, and what happened. Screenshots help a lot.
  • 💡 Tool suggestions — Have an idea for a new tool? We're always looking for utilities that would benefit from running client-side. If it can be built in the browser, we want to hear about it.
  • 📝 Feedback — Like something? Hate something? Think a tool could work differently? Your perspective helps us improve.
  • 🤝 Partnerships & press — Interested in writing about SimpleUtils or collaborating? Reach out and let's talk.

A Note on Privacy

Consistent with our approach to tools, we respect your inbox too. We'll never add you to a mailing list, sell your contact information, or send unsolicited messages. If you email us, we'll reply and that's it.
