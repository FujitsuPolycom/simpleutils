Why does this matter?

Most "free" tools aren't really free — you pay with your data, your attention, and your privacy. Sign up for an account and you've handed over your email. Use a tool and trackers note what you did, when, and how often.

SimpleUtils exists to be genuinely free. Open the page, use the tool, leave. That's it.

What do we actually store?

The only things stored on your machine are:

  • ✓ Cached AI model files — stored in your browser's cache so you don't have to re-download them. You can clear these any time via your browser settings.
  • ✓ Nothing else. No session data, no preferences, no history.

Verify it yourself

Open your browser's developer tools (F12), go to the Network tab, and use any tool. You'll see:

  • No requests to analytics domains (google-analytics.com, etc.)
  • No POST requests containing your data
  • No third-party tracking pixels
